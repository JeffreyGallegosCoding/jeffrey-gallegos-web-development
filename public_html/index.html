Whats For Lunch

WhatsForLunch is an application designed for future DiveDive students to efficiently find restaurants within a one mile radius of the Stemulus Center.

The main page of the site displays a map of the restaurants in the area around the stemulus center with each restaurant having a pin to mark their location. If the user wishes to create a profile, they have the option to create a favorites list for all of their favorite restaurants in the area. This allows the user easy access to each of the restaurant's information and links to the restaurant's yelp pages. Once a restaurant is in the favorites menu, the map even updates with a colored pin to show a favorited restaurant's location more conveniently on the map.

Since the deep dive students are only in the program for a few months, this application will allow them to get to know more of the establishments in the downtown area that they may not have tried. Our group has even included some of our favorite restaurants that we have visited during the program, if they would like an idea of where they could go first.
